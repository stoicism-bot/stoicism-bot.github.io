add What are the main principles of Stoicism?

The main principles of Stoicism include:

  • Focus on what you can control
  • Live in accordance with reason and virtue
  • Practice self-discipline and self-control
  • Embrace challenges as opportunities for growth
  • Cultivate inner peace and tranquility
